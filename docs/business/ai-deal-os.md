AI Deal OS is the commercial wedge for Aun Stack.

It turns AI second-opinion work, AI project diagnosis, proposal review, scope review, estimate review, and implementation planning into productized AgentOps workflows.

## Why This Fits Aun Stack

Aun Stack is an enterprise AgentOps control plane for multi-agent AI workflows.

The current commercial market needs more than AI prompts. AI implementation companies need a controlled way to manage:

- project context
- decisions and assumptions
- human approvals
- scope boundaries
- risk reviews
- handoffs between sales and delivery
- audit trails
- next implementation steps

These are exactly the problems Aun Stack is designed to solve.

## Target Customers

Initial target customers:

- AI implementation companies
- DX consulting companies
- software development companies
- Web production companies moving into AI
- kintone / Google Workspace / Microsoft 365 implementation partners
- SaaS companies adding AI services
- SIers and regional IT companies

They already have customers and sales access, but may lack reliable AI project review and delivery control.

## Pain

These companies face the following problems:

- AI projects are easy to sell but hard to scope
- customer expectations are often unrealistic
- data readiness is unclear
- RAG, API, SaaS, agent, and manual operations are confused
- estimate items are missed
- human approval and audit requirements are not considered
- PoC and production operation are mixed
- sales promises do not transfer cleanly to delivery teams

## Productization Path

### Phase 1: Manual Diagnosis

Use templates and human review.

Deliver:

- diagnosis report
- question list
- estimate checklist
- scope boundary memo

### Phase 2: Structured Workflow

Turn repeated patterns into structured forms and markdown workflows.

Deliver:

- intake schema
- risk taxonomy
- estimate item library
- approval gate rules
- report template

### Phase 3: Assisted AgentOps

Use Aun Stack modules to assist the workflow.

Map modules:

| Aun Stack Module | AI Deal OS Role |
|---|---|
| Aun Relay | Coordinate human and agent discussion around a deal |
| Aun Router | Route review tasks to the right agent or expert |
| Aun Gate | Enforce pricing, claims, scope, and human approval gates |
| Kusabi Memory | Anchor assumptions, decisions, and customer constraints |
| Tasuki Handoff | Move from sales review to delivery planning safely |
| Shirube Structure | Structure features, tasks, estimates, and dependencies |
| Hibiki Context | Re-present deal context for review, proposal, and delivery |

### Phase 4: Partner OS

Offer the system to AI implementation companies as a recurring operating layer.

Possible offers:

- AI Deal Review Subscription
- AI Proposal Review Desk
- AI Implementation Partner OS
- Weekly AI Deal CTO
- AI Deal OS for DX Firms
