## Customer Pain

Many companies can sell AI projects, but struggle with questions such as:

- Is this project technically feasible?
- Should this be built with RAG, MCP, API integration, SaaS automation, or manual workflow design?
- Is the customer expectation too high?
- What discovery questions are missing?
- What should be excluded from scope?
- Where is human approval required?
- What permissions and audit logs are needed?
- What estimate items are often forgotten?

## Diagnosis Framework

### 1. Business Goal
- What business outcome is expected?
- Is the success metric measurable?
- Is the customer asking for AI, or for a business result?

### 2. Data Reality
- What data exists?
- Where is it stored?
- Who owns it?
- Is it clean enough?
- Are there access restrictions?

### 3. Technical Fit
- Plain LLM prompt
- RAG
- MCP
- API integration
- workflow automation
- custom app
- human-assisted operation

### 4. Risk
- hallucination
- permission leak
- over-automation
- user expectation gap
- missing human approval
- lack of audit trail
- operational owner missing

### 5. Scope
- what should be done in PoC
- what should wait until production
- what should remain human-controlled
- what should be excluded
