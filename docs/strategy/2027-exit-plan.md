# 2027 Exit Plan

## Goal

By June 2027, Aun Stack should reach a state where it can reasonably be sold, partially sold, strategically invested in, or retained as a profitable AI AgentOps business.

The initial minimum target is a potential 100 million JPY exit. The larger objective is to create enough momentum that selling for only 100 million JPY feels optional rather than necessary.

## Exit Asset

The exit asset is **Aun Stack**, an enterprise AgentOps control plane for multi-agent AI workflows.

Aun Stack is not a single MCP server. It is the management layer for enterprises using multiple AI agents and tools.

It manages:

- agent selection and routing
- project context
- decisions and assumptions
- PR continuity
- safe handoffs
- human approvals
- permissions
- audit logs
- enterprise workflow control

## Strategic Thesis

AI coding agents and personal agents will become stronger quickly. That makes individual coding or workflow automation tools easier to replace.

The durable opportunity is the layer enterprises need around those agents:

- control
- auditability
- permissioning
- handoff
- continuity
- governance
- human-in-the-loop operation

Aun Stack should not compete directly with Codex, Claude Code, Cursor, Gemini, or Copilot. It should make them usable in enterprise workflows.

## Exit Options

### 1. Full sale
Sell Aun Stack business and related assets if a buyer values the product, customers, and founder capability highly.

### 2. Partial sale
Sell a minority or majority stake while retaining upside.

### 3. Strategic capital
Bring in a strategic investor with distribution, customers, or engineering support.

### 4. Retain and grow
Keep the business if MRR and growth justify holding.

## Operating Principle

**Build to sell. Operate to keep.**

Aun Stack should be built with clean documentation, customer records, repeatable offers, and technical assets so it can be acquired. At the same time, the business should be profitable enough that selling is optional.

## Non-Negotiables

- Avoid low-priced ChatGPT training.
- Avoid vague custom AI development.
- Avoid heavy implementation that does not feed Aun Stack.
- Avoid high-risk medical, legal, or financial decision automation.
- Keep every revenue engagement connected to Aun Stack learning, case studies, or buyer access.
