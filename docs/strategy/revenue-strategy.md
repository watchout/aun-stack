# Revenue Strategy

## Purpose

Aun Stack must generate revenue during development without trapping the founder in custom implementation work.

The short-term revenue strategy funds product development and creates real customer insight, case studies, buyer relationships, and AgentOps requirements.

## Core Rule

Revenue work is acceptable only when it strengthens Aun Stack.

Accept work that creates at least one of the following:

- AgentOps requirements
- buyer relationships
- case studies
- recurring revenue
- productizable workflow
- credibility with AI/DX/R&D companies

Avoid work that creates only custom labor.

## Primary Offers

### 1. AI Project Second Opinion

Price: 100,000 - 300,000 JPY per project

Target customers:

- AI implementation companies
- DX consulting companies
- web production companies
- IT subsidy support companies
- software development companies

Value:

Review an AI project before or during proposal to reduce implementation risk.

Deliverables:

- project risk assessment
- feasibility notes
- MCP / RAG / API / SaaS fit analysis
- permission and human approval recommendations
- scope warnings
- required discovery questions
- estimate items to include

### 2. AI-Driven Development Process Diagnosis

Price: 200,000 - 500,000 JPY

Target customers:

- software development companies
- SaaS companies
- AI development teams
- teams adopting Codex, Claude Code, Cursor, or similar agents

Value:

Help teams use AI coding agents with smaller PRs, better review flow, and stronger continuity.

Deliverables:

- current development workflow assessment
- AI agent usage map
- small PR strategy
- review workflow design
- context handoff design
- GitHub issue/PR template recommendations
- initial AgentOps roadmap

### 3. Weekly AI Implementation Lead

Price: 800,000 - 1,000,000 JPY per month

Target customers:

- AI implementation companies
- DX companies
- R&D companies
- SaaS companies
- companies with AI initiatives but no clear implementation lead

Value:

Weekly executive/technical leadership for AI implementation without taking on full custom development.

Scope:

- weekly onsite or executive meeting
- AI project review
- AI implementation roadmap
- agent/tool selection
- proposal review
- implementation team direction
- AgentOps design
- monthly executive report

Out of scope unless separately contracted:

- full implementation
- production support
- 24/7 incident response
- regulated clinical/legal/financial decision automation
- vague custom AI development

- 10 high-quality contacts per week
- 2-3 discovery conversations per week

- 1 strategic partner conversation per month

## Qualification Criteria

Good opportunities:

- customer already has AI demand
- customer has budget
- work can be scoped as diagnosis, design, review, or leadership
- work produces Aun Stack insight
- founder time is limited and high-value

Bad opportunities:

- asks for generic ChatGPT training
- wants unlimited Q&A
- asks for unspecified AI development
- requires daily low-level implementation
- depends on regulated expert judgment
- does not connect to Aun Stack or buyer access

## Founder Time Rule

The founder should not spend more than 1-2 days per week on revenue work once product development is active.

The ideal model is:

- 1 day/week: high-value advisory or weekly AI implementation lead
- 1 day/week: sales, proposals, and relationship building
- 3 days/week: Aun Stack and related product development
